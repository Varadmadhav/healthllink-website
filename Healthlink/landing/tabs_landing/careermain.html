Lab Technician

Join our state-of-the-art laboratory team and play a crucial role in diagnostic testing. Work with advanced equipment and contribute to accurate patient diagnoses in our NABL and CAP certified facilities.

Key Requirements:

  • Diploma/Degree in Medical Laboratory Technology
  • Knowledge of laboratory procedures and equipment
  • Attention to detail and accuracy
  • Understanding of quality control measures
  • Computer literacy and data entry skills
🩸

Sample Collection Specialist

Become a vital link between patients and our laboratory services. Handle sample collection with precision and care, ensuring patient comfort while maintaining the highest standards of safety and hygiene.

Key Requirements:

  • Training in phlebotomy and sample collection
  • Excellent patient interaction skills
  • Knowledge of safety protocols
  • Ability to handle various sample types
  • Strong organizational skills
🏠

Home Visit Coordinator

Provide convenient healthcare services directly to patients' homes. Coordinate and conduct home visits for sample collection, bringing our quality diagnostic services to the comfort of patients' homes.

Key Requirements:

  • Experience in home healthcare services
  • Valid driving license and own vehicle
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Flexible working hours capability
  • Knowledge of local area and navigation
