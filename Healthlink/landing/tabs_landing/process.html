Join Our Team

Your journey to a rewarding career in healthcare diagnostics starts here. Follow our simple application process to become part of the HealthLink family.

1

Choose Your Position

Browse our available positions and select the role that matches your skills and career goals. We offer opportunities in laboratory technology, sample collection, and home healthcare services.

Available Positions:

  • Lab Technician - NABL & CAP certified facilities
  • Sample Collection Specialist - Patient-focused care
  • Home Visit Coordinator - Convenient healthcare services
2

Prepare Your Documents

Gather all necessary documents before starting your application. Having everything ready will make the process smooth and efficient.

Required Documents:

  • Updated resume/CV with relevant experience
  • Educational certificates and transcripts
  • Professional certifications (if applicable)
  • Valid ID proof and address proof
  • Reference letters from previous employers
  • Recent passport-size photographs
3

Submit Online Application

Complete our comprehensive online application form. Take your time to provide accurate information and highlight your relevant experience and skills.

Application Sections:

  • Personal information and contact details
  • Educational background and qualifications
  • Work experience and achievements
  • Skills assessment and certifications
  • Availability and salary expectations
  • Upload supporting documents
4

Initial Screening

Our HR team will review your application and conduct an initial screening to assess your qualifications and fit for the position.

Screening Process:

  • Application review and document verification
  • Phone Call interview
  • Skills and competency assessment
  • Background and reference checks
  • Feedback and next steps communication
5

Technical Interview

Qualified candidates will be invited for a technical interview with our department heads to assess specific skills and knowledge relevant to the position.

Interview Focus Areas:

  • Technical knowledge and expertise
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Patient interaction skills
  • Safety protocols and procedures
  • Quality control measures
  • Teamwork and communication
6

Final Selection & Onboarding

Successful candidates will receive a job offer and begin our comprehensive onboarding process to ensure a smooth transition into their new role.

Onboarding Includes:

  • Contract signing and documentation
  • Orientation and company introduction
  • Training on systems and procedures
  • Safety and compliance training
  • Mentorship assignment
  • Performance goal setting
