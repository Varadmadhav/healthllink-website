Take a look at our Corporate Health Check Up Offerings

Annual Health Checks

Our Annual Health Check Up Programs are tailored to provide a comprehensive health screening for corporates. From vital organ function assessments to blood profiles and specialised tests, our programs cover a spectrum of health indicators.

Drug Testing

Drug testing is a very important pre-employment health check up. Our Drug Testing for Employees program is designed to support employers in promoting a safe and drug-free workplace.

Pre-Employment Health Checks

Our Pre-Employment Health Checks are designed to help corporates with their hiring process. These pre-employment screenings provide insights into the health of prospective employees helping organisations in maintaining a healthy workforce.

Corporate Health Camps

Corporate Health Camps will be set up in your office premises throughout the day. It's a great way to check for employees' health without disrupting their day-to-day office work. They can visit the camp as per their work schedule.

Pre-Policy Medical Checks

Our Pre-Policy Health Checks for Insurance providers offer a thorough and efficient solution, enabling insurance providers to make informed decisions while ensuring individuals are adequately covered.

Marketing Tie-Ups for Pharmaceutical Industry

We specialise in screening camps with a high fulfilment rate for brand promotions and fostering meaningful engagement between Healthcare Professionals and Pharma brands. Also, we provide adherence programs that support patients throughout clinical diagnosis, doctor consultations, and therapy management.
