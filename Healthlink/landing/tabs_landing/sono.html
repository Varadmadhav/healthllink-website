What sonography services are available at HealthLink?

We offer a comprehensive range of sonography services including abdominal ultrasound, pelvic scanning, pregnancy monitoring, thyroid ultrasound, cardiac echo, and specialized diagnostic imaging for accurate health assessment.

2

How does sonography help in medical diagnosis?

Sonography uses high-frequency sound waves to create detailed images of internal organs, tissues, and blood flow. It helps diagnose conditions in the abdomen, pelvis, heart, blood vessels, and monitor pregnancy development safely without radiation exposure.

3

What are the benefits of ultrasound imaging?

Ultrasound imaging is non-invasive, painless, and radiation-free. It provides real-time imaging, helps guide procedures, monitors treatment progress, and is particularly valuable for pregnancy care, organ assessment, and detecting abnormalities early.

4

How quickly can you receive your sonography results?

Sonography results are typically available within 2 to 4 hours after the scan. For routine examinations, reports are provided the same day. Emergency cases receive immediate preliminary findings with detailed reports following within 24 hours.
