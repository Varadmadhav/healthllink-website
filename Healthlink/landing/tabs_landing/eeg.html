Learn More About EEG/EMG Services

1
What EEG/EMG services are available at HealthLink?
We offer comprehensive neurological testing services including EEG (Electroencephalogram) for brain activity monitoring and EMG (Electromyography) for muscle and nerve function assessment.
2
How can EEG/EMG help in diagnosing neurological conditions?
EEG/EMG tests help in diagnosing neurological conditions by measuring electrical activity in the brain and muscles. These tests can detect seizures, epilepsy, sleep disorders, muscle weakness, and nerve damage.
3
How do EEG/EMG tests contribute to neurological treatment planning?
EEG/EMG tests play a crucial role in neurological treatment by providing detailed information about brain wave patterns and muscle function. This helps doctors plan appropriate treatments, monitor disease progression, and evaluate treatment effectiveness for conditions like epilepsy, neuropathy, and myopathy.
4
How quickly can you receive your EEG/EMG results?
EEG/EMG results typically range from 24 to 48 hours, depending on the complexity of the test and the urgency of the case. Preliminary findings may be available immediately after the test.
