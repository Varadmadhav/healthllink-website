What pathology services are available at HealthLink?

We offer a comprehensive range of pathology services including blood chemistry, hematology, microbiology, immunology, histopathology, and specialized molecular diagnostics for accurate disease detection and monitoring.

2

How can pathology help in diagnosing diseases and health conditions?

Pathology helps in diagnosing diseases by analyzing body fluids, tissues, and cells using advanced laboratory techniques. It provides crucial information about infections, metabolic disorders, cancers, and other medical conditions through precise testing methods.

3

How does pathology contribute to preventive healthcare and treatment monitoring?

Pathology plays a vital role in preventive healthcare by enabling early detection of diseases through routine screenings and health check-ups. It also monitors treatment effectiveness, tracks disease progression, and helps physicians make informed decisions about patient care and medication adjustments.

4

How quickly can you receive your pathology results?

Pathology results typically range from 2 hours to 3 days, depending on the complexity of the test. Routine blood tests are usually available within 4-6 hours, while specialized tests like biopsies may take 2-3 days. Emergency tests are processed immediately with results available within 1-2 hours.
