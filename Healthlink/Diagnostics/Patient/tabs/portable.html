What portable X-ray services are available at HealthLink?
We offer comprehensive portable X-ray services that come to your location, including chest X-rays, bone X-rays, and joint imaging for accurate diagnosis at your convenience.
2
How can portable X-ray help in diagnosing health conditions?
Portable X-ray helps diagnose various conditions including fractures, chest infections, pneumonia, and bone abnormalities by providing clear imaging at your home or office without compromising on quality.
3
What are the benefits of choosing portable X-ray services?
Portable X-ray services offer convenience, reduced travel time, comfort of familiar surroundings, and are especially beneficial for elderly patients, disabled individuals, or those with mobility issues who find it difficult to visit diagnostic centers.
4
How quickly can you receive your portable X-ray results?
Portable X-ray results are typically available within 24 to 48 hours after the imaging is completed. Digital reports can be shared via email or WhatsApp for immediate access.
