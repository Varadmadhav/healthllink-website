What sonography services are available at HealthLink?
We offer comprehensive sonography services including abdominal ultrasound, pelvic ultrasound, obstetric scans, and Doppler studies for detailed imaging and accurate diagnosis.
2
How does sonography help in medical diagnosis?
Sonography uses high-frequency sound waves to create real-time images of internal organs, tissues, and blood flow, helping doctors diagnose conditions without radiation exposure.
3
What is the importance of sonography during pregnancy?
Prenatal sonography is crucial for monitoring fetal development, detecting abnormalities, determining due dates, and ensuring both mother and baby's health throughout pregnancy. It provides safe, real-time visualization without harmful radiation.
4
How quickly can you receive your sonography results?
Sonography results are typically available within 2-4 hours after the scan, with emergency cases receiving immediate preliminary reports. Final detailed reports are usually ready within 24 hours.
