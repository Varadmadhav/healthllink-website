How can cardiology help in diagnosing and identifying heart conditions?

Cardiology helps in diagnosing and identifying heart conditions by using advanced diagnostic techniques to visualize heart structures, assess blood flow, monitor heart rhythm, and evaluate overall cardiac function.

3

How does cardiology contribute to heart disease prevention and treatment?

Cardiology plays a crucial role in heart disease prevention and treatment by using diagnostic imaging to identify cardiovascular conditions early, monitor disease progression, and assess treatment effectiveness. It guides interventional procedures and helps in planning comprehensive cardiac care strategies.

4

How quickly can you receive your cardiology results?

Cardiology results typically range from 2 to 24 hours, depending on the complexity of the test and the urgency of the case. Emergency cardiac assessments are processed immediately.
