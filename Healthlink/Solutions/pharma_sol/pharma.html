About Us

Established in 2013, HealthLink Diagnostics & Solutions Pvt Ltd has been dedicated to providing accurate, reliable, and affordable diagnostic services to our community and corporate partners. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted healthcare partner for thousands of patients, doctors, hospitals, and organizations seeking comprehensive wellness solutions for their teams. At HealthLink, we combine cutting-edge technology with highly qualified professionals to deliver precise test results and actionable health insights you can rely on. Our modern labs, extensive range of diagnostic tests, and unwavering commitment to quality have earned us a reputation for excellence in patient care and corporate health management. Your health is our priority — we ensure that every test is conducted with the utmost care, confidentiality, and speed, empowering you and your doctor to make informed decisions. From routine health check-ups and specialized testing to preventive care packages and tailor-made corporate wellness programs, HealthLink is your one-stop solution for all diagnostic and employee health needs. We specialise in screening camps with a high fulfilment rate for brand promotions and fostering meaningful engagement between Healthcare Professionals and Pharma brands. Also, we provide adherence programs that support patients throughout clinical diagnosis, doctor consultations, and therapy management. Join the thousands who trust us for their personal and workplace health journey — because at HealthLink, we are your trusted healthcare partner for life. Since our founding, we have grown into a diverse team of experts committed to excellence, innovation, and putting our clients first, whether individuals or businesses.
